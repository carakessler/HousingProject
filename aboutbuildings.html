Welcome to Jackson Court!

Wilder and Anderson Towers

Home to many special interest housing groups, Anderson and Wilder have mostly a suite style setup. Suites typically contain 4 singles, 1 double, your own suite lounge, and one bathroom that is cleaned once a week by the ESW. There are also Center Doubles. Two doubles are joined by a shared bathroom (cleaned weekly), but with no lounge. There is a shared kitchen for every floor.Students can be on a Group 3 meal plan if they like.

O'Brien

Our newest building is 5 stories tall with the first floor devoted to large public spaces open for group use. Floors 2-5 house students and have a mixture of singles and doubles plus center doubles (shared bath) for groups of four students. Each room is carpeted, air conditioned, and double rooms have sinks. Each floor has a large community lounge and a well equipped study room.

Lovejoy (floors 1, 3, 4) Burton and Crosby are the options for returning students. All buildings have long corridors, lined with communal style bathrooms, mostly double rooms and a few singles. The Quad is obviously the closest to classes, a real plus for some students. The doubles in Lovejoy are pretty big, singles are small. The doubles in Burton and Crosby are a bit smaller than other quad doubles but have the added bonus of an in-room sink.

The Greek Quad is mainly special interest, with most of the buildings being home to fraternities. There are also non-Greek special interest housing options, such as the Douglass Leadership House and the Drama House, but those both go through a separate online application. Through this lottery it is possible to live the the Psi U House in spacious singles or doubles.
x

Juniors and seniors can consider the Maisonettes (2 person apartments), which are the small houses at the base of the two towers. They include two bedrooms (one is large and the other is on the small side). There is a furnished living room and full kitchen. The bathroom is shared. deKiewiet and Valentine have 2 and 3 bedroom apartments with a full kitchen and a bathroom shared by apartment mates. In the 3 room apartments, there are two large bedrooms and a small bedroom, all singles. Housing in deKiewiet and Valentine is $200 less per semester. Residents are responsible for their own for cleaning.
x

Riverview is only open to juniors and seniors. There are 4 person apartments or a 2 person apartments, a large common space, and a full kitchens with stoves, microwaves, refrigerators, dishwashers. There is air conditioning and each room has its own bathroom! Residents are responsible for their own for cleaning.
